Wooden Snowflakes

  • Set of 15 wooden snowflakes
  • Laser cut in 3 mm plywood
  • Dimensions: 60 x 60 mm.
  • Possibility to create unique models

About the product

Do you know that snowflakes are unique? From this fact I decided to make a snowflake generator that randomly creates 3d models.

You can make your own or order the models you want and I will make them for you. They are cut with a laser into 3 mm plywood or plexiglass.
